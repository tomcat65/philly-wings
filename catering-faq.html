Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about catering with us

How far in advance do I need to order?

We require 24 hours advance notice for all catering orders. For orders over 100 people, we recommend 48 hours notice to ensure we can accommodate your event.

What is your delivery area?

We deliver within a 10-mile radius of our Oxford Circle location. This covers most of NE Philadelphia including Frankford, Tacony, Bridesburg, Port Richmond, Kensington, Mayfair, Holmesburg, Torresdale, Fishtown, Northern Liberties, South Philly, Northern Liberties, Bucks County, Lower Northeast, Germantown, East Falls, Bristol, and surrounding neighborhoods.

How does payment work?

All orders are processed securely through Stripe. You'll complete payment directly on their platform, and they provide 24/7 customer support for any payment questions.

Can I customize sauce selections?

Absolutely! Each package includes a specific number of sauce selections. You can choose any combination from our 14 signature sauces. We recommend mixing heat levels to accommodate everyone on your team.

What if I need to change my order?

Contact customer support as soon as possible. Changes can typically be made up to 24 hours before your delivery time.

Do you provide serving supplies?

Yes! All packages include plates, napkins, wet wipes, and serving utensils. We also label each sauce container so your team knows what they're getting.

Can I order for dietary restrictions?

Our boneless wings can accommodate most dietary needs. Contact us directly at (267) 376-3113 to discuss specific allergen concerns. All our nutrition information is available on our website.

What if I need to feed more than 100 people?

We love large events! Contact us directly at (267) 376-3113 and we'll work with you to create a custom package that fits your needs.
